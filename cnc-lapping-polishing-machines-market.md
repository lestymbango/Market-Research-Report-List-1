<p><p>CNC lapping polishing machines cater to two main market types: single surface lapping polishing and double surface lapping polishing. Single surface lapping focuses on achieving precision finishes on one side of a workpiece, ideal for applications requiring tight tolerances. In contrast, double surface lapping simultaneously processes both sides, enhancing efficiency and uniformity, particularly for components needing balanced specifications. Both types are essential in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, optics, and aerospace, where quality and precision are paramount.</p></p>
