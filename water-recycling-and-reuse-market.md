<p><strong>The Water Recycling and Reuse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Treatment Technology</li><li>Chemical Treatment Technology</li><li>Biological Treatment Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water recycling and reuse involve three primary market types: </p><p>1. **Physical Treatment Technology** focuses on processes like filtration and sedimentation to remove solids and particulates from water.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Chemical Treatment Technology** uses chemicals to treat water by disinfection, coagulation, or oxidation, effectively eliminating contaminants and pathogens.</p><p>3. **Biological Treatment Technology** employs microorganisms to break down organic matter, enhancing water quality through processes like aerobic and anaerobic digestion. Together, these technologies enable effective recycling and reuse of water across various applications.</p></p>
