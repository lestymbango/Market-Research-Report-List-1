<p><strong>The Cooled Infrared Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cooled Long-wave Infrared Camera</li><li>Cooled Middle-wave Infrared Camera</li><li>Cooled Short-wave Infrared Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooled infrared camera market is segmented into three main types based on their wavelength sensitivity. Cooled long-wave infrared cameras (LWIR) detect thermal radiation, making them ideal for applications like surveillance and industrial monitoring. Cooled middle-wave infrared cameras (MWIR) are used in gas detection and military applications due to their superior resolution and sensitivity. Cooled short-wave infrared cameras (SWIR) capture reflections and are effective in assessing material properties and detecting moisture, enhancing applications in agriculture and semiconductor inspection.</p></p>
