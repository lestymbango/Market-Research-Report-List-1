<p><p>Coronary balloon catheters are medical devices used in percutaneous coronary interventions (PCIs) to treat narrowed or obstructed coronary arteries. <p><p>The coronary balloon catheters market includes several types tailored for specific procedures. Aspiration catheters are designed to clear blockages by removing thrombus from coronary arteries during percutaneous interventions. Diagnostic catheters facilitate the assessment of coronary artery conditions through imaging and pressure measurements, helping guide treatment decisions. Guide catheters serve to navigate and position other devices within the vascular system, ensuring accuracy during interventions. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing the efficacy and safety of cardiovascular procedures.</p></p>
