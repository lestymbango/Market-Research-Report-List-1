<p><strong>The Cannabis Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chromatography Based Analyzer</li><li>Spectroscopy Based Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cannabis Testing Equipment market is primarily classified into two types: chromatography-based analyzers and spectroscopy-based analyzers. Chromatography-based analyzers, such as Gas Chromatography (GC) and High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), are utilized for separating and quantifying cannabinoids and terpenes in cannabis samples. In contrast, spectroscopy-based analyzers, including Near-Infrared (NIR) and Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) spectrometers, provide rapid qualitative analysis by measuring the interaction of light with the sample. Both types ensure compliance with regulatory standards and enhance product quality.</p></p>
