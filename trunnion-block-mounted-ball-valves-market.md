<p><p>Trunnion block-mounted ball valves are crucial components in fluid control systems, characterized by their support structure and operation mechanism. The full bore type allows unrestricted flow, featuring a straight-through design that minimizes pressure drops and turbulence, making it ideal for high-flow applications. Conversely, reduced bore valves have a smaller internal diameter, which results in higher pressure drops and velocity increase but can optimize space and weight in certain systems. Both types cater to distinct operational needs within various industries.</p></p>
