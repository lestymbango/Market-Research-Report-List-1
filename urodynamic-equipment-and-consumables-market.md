Electromyographs assess pelvic floor muscle activity, and video urodynamic systems combine imaging with pressure measurements for detailed analysis. Urodynamic consumables include catheters and sensors essential for these tests. Other market segments may include specialized devices and software that enhance urodynamic evaluations.</p></p>
