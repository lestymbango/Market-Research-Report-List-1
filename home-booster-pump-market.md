<p><p>The Home Booster Pump market is categorized into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for low-pressure applications, providing adequate pressure for small households with minimal height differences. In contrast, multi-stage pumps are suitable for higher pressure needs, ideal for larger homes or those with more significant elevation changes. Multi-stage pumps can efficiently elevate water pressure over multiple tiers, making them a preferred choice for extensive plumbing systems requiring sustained high pressure.</p></p>
