<p><p>The sedimentation thickener market is primarily divided into two types: Column Type and Bridge Type. Column Type thickeners utilize a tall, vertical design, allowing for efficient separation of solids from liquids due to gravity, making them suitable for space-constrained environments. Conversely, Bridge Type thickeners feature a horizontal design supported by a bridge-like structure, enabling larger throughput and better handling of varied solid concentrations. Both types serve crucial roles in industrial processes, especially in mining, wastewater treatment, and chemical production.</p></p>
