<p><p>The surgical wound closure devices market includes closure strips, tissue adhesives, and sutures, each serving unique roles in wound management. Closure strips are adhesive strips used to bring wound edges together, promoting healing without penetrating the skin. Tissue adhesives, like cyanoacrylates, bond skin edges swiftly, providing a secure seal while minimizing infection risk. Sutures are traditional methods using thread to stitch tissue, offering precise closure but requiring proper technique for optimal results. These devices address diverse surgical needs and enhance patient outcomes.</p></p>
