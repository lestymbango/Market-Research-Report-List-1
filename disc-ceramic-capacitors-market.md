<p><p>Disc ceramic capacitors are essential components in electronic circuits, primarily differentiated by their lead types. Straight leads type capacitors feature leads that extend directly from the body, making them ideal for through-hole mount applications. In contrast, crimped leads type capacitors have leads that are bent or crimped, allowing for easier positioning and soldering on printed circuit boards. Both types cater to diverse market needs in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, driven by their reliability and efficiency in filtering and energy storage.</p></p>
