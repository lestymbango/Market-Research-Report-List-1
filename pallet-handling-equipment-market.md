<p><p>The pallet handling equipment market can be categorized into automatic and manual types. Automatic pallet handling equipment includes systems like automated guided vehicles (AGVs) and palletizers that streamline operations with minimal human intervention, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs. In contrast, manual pallet handling equipment involves tools like pallet jacks and forklifts, requiring operator input for movement and stacking.
