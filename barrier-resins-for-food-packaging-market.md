<p><p>Barrier resins in food packaging are crucial for preventing oxygen, moisture, and flavors from affecting product quality. EVOH (ethylene-vinyl alcohol) resin offers excellent oxygen barrier properties and is widely used for perishable items. PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) is known for its outstanding moisture resistance, making it ideal for packaging sensitive products. PEN (polyethylene naphthalate) combines thermal stability and superior barrier performance, suitable for high-temperature applications. Other barrier resins might include biodegradable options or blends tailored for specific packaging needs.</p></p>
