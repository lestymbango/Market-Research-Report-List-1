<p><strong>The Thinset Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-set Mortar</li><li>Latex-portland Cement Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thinset mortar market is primarily categorized into two types: dry-set mortar and latex-portland cement mortar. Dry-set mortar, a cement-based product, requires water for activation, offering strong adhesion for tile installation. It’s widely used in residential and commercial projects. In contrast, latex-portland cement mortar incorporates latex additives to enhance flexibility and bonding strength, making it ideal for installations subject to movement or moisture. Both types serve distinct applications in construction, catering to varied needs of durability and performance.</p></p>
