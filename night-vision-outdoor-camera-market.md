<p><p>The Night Vision Outdoor Camera market features two primary types: Fixed Cameras and PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) Cameras. In contrast, PTZ Cameras offer enhanced flexibility, allowing users to remotely pan, tilt, and zoom in on areas of interest.
