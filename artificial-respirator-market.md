<p><strong>The Artificial Respirator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Pressure Type</li><li>Constant Volume Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial respirator market includes two primary types: constant pressure and constant volume respirators. Constant pressure respirators maintain a steady airway pressure, ensuring effective ventilation regardless of lung compliance changes. These are often used in critical care settings. In contrast, constant volume respirators deliver a specific volume of air per breath, regardless of the pressure variations in the patient's lungs. Each type is designed to meet different patient needs and clinical scenarios, enhancing respiratory support in various medical environments.</p></p>
