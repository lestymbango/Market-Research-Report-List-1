<p><ul><li>Coordinate Measuring Machine</li><li>Optical Measuring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geometry measuring machine market is primarily divided into two main types: Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) and Optical Measuring Machines. CMMs utilize a probe to touch various points on an object, providing precise dimensional data, ideal for complex geometries. In contrast, Optical Measuring Machines use light, typically laser or cameras, to capture measurements without contact, making them suitable for delicate or reflective surfaces. Both types enhance quality control and precision in manufacturing processes across various industries.</p></p>
