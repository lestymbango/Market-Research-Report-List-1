<p><strong>The Welding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Welding</li><li>Electrogas/Electroslag Welding</li><li>Friction Welding</li><li>Laser & Electron-Beam Welding</li><li>Resistance Welding</li><li>Oxy-Fuel Welding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding equipment market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Arc welding utilizes electrical arcs to melt materials for strong joins. Electrogas and electroslag welding combine molten metal and gravity for thick welds. Friction welding generates heat through friction to bond materials. Laser and electron-beam welding employ focused beams for precision welding. Resistance welding uses heat generated by electrical resistance to fuse materials. Oxy-fuel welding combines oxygen and fuel gases for cutting and welding. Other methods include specialized techniques for niche applications.</p></p>
