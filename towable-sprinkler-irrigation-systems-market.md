<p><p>Towable sprinkler irrigation systems encompass various types, each suited for different agricultural needs. **Center Pivot** systems rotate around a central pivot, allowing uniform coverage of large fields. **Lateral Move** systems consist of long pipes that move laterally across the field, providing flexibility for smaller areas. **Solid Set** systems feature fixed pipes and sprinklers, ideal for permanent installations with consistent irrigation. **Others** include specialized configurations and innovations tailored to specific crops, terrains, or conditions, enhancing efficiency and water management.</p></p>
