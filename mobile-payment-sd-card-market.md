<p><p>The mobile payment SD card market can be categorized into two main types: independence and inlay. Independent SD cards are standalone products with integrated payment functionalities that can be used in compatible devices without additional hardware, offering flexibility to users. In contrast, inlay cards incorporate payment technology directly into the card structure, typically used in NFC-enabled devices or smart cards. Both types enhance the convenience of mobile payments, catering to diverse consumer needs and technological preferences in the market.</p></p>
