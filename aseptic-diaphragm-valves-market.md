<p><p>Aseptic diaphragm valves are specialized components used in sterile processes, particularly in the pharmaceutical and food industries. The market is segmented into 2-way and 3-way valves. 2-way valves are designed to control the flow of fluid in a single line, allowing for straightforward on/off functionality.
