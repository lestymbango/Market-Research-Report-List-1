<p><p>The preemergent herbicide market consists of two main types: selective and non-selective herbicides. Selective herbicides target specific weed species while leaving desired plants unharmed, making them ideal for use in lawns and gardens. Non-selective herbicides, on the other hand, eliminate all plant growth, including desirable vegetation, and are often used for total weed control in industrial areas and pathways. Both types are crucial for effective weed management, with applications varying based on environmental and agricultural needs.</p></p>
