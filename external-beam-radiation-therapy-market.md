<p><ul><li>Conformal Radiation Therapy</li><li>Proton Beam Therapy</li><li>Image Guided Radiation Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>External beam radiation therapy (EBRT) encompasses various advanced techniques to target tumors effectively while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Conformal radiation therapy shapes the radiation beams to match the contours of the tumor, enhancing precision. Proton beam therapy utilizes protons instead of X-rays, allowing for targeted energy delivery and reduced exposure to healthy tissue. Image-guided radiation therapy integrates imaging technology to improve treatment accuracy, enabling real-time adjustments to tumor positioning, ultimately enhancing treatment outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
