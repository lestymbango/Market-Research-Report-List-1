<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Electronic</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft starter market is categorized into three main types: mechanical, electronic, and mixed type. Mechanical soft starters use physical devices like series resistors to limit the inrush current during motor startup, providing a simple and cost-effective solution. Electronic soft starters utilize advanced circuitry to control voltage and current, offering greater precision and efficiency with features like torque control. Mixed type soft starters combine elements of both mechanical and electronic systems, balancing cost and performance for versatile applications across various industries.</p></p>
