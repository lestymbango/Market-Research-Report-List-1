Elastomeric membranes are flexible and rubber-like, providing excellent resistance to UV radiation and temperature fluctuations, ideal for diverse climates. Cementitious membranes consist of a cement-based mix, providing strong waterproofing and protective qualities, often used in areas requiring additional fire resistance.
