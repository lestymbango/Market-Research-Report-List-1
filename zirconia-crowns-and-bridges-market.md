<p><p>Zirconia crowns and bridges are popular dental restorations known for their strength and aesthetic appeal. The market includes two main types: layered zirconia and monolithic zirconia. Layered zirconia crowns and bridges feature a core of zirconia covered with a porcelain layer, enhancing aesthetics while retaining durability. Monolithic zirconia, on the other hand, is made entirely from zirconia, offering high strength and reduced wear on opposing teeth. Both types are used to restore damaged teeth and improve patient satisfaction in dental treatments.</p></p>
