<p><p>The biventricular pacemaker market is categorized into temporary and permanent pacemakers. Temporary pacemakers are used for short-term cardiac support, often in acute settings, allowing for the stabilization of patients until a more permanent solution is required. In contrast, permanent pacemakers are designed for long-term use, providing continuous cardiac stimulation for patients with chronic heart failure or arrhythmias. Both types aim to improve cardiac function, enhancing patient quality of life and reducing hospital readmissions.</p></p>
