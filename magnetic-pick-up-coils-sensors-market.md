<p><p>Magnetic pick-up coils sensors are categorized into several types based on their operating principles. Hall Effect sensors detect magnetic fields by generating a voltage in response to the field’s presence. Magnetoresistive sensors leverage changes in resistance in magnetic fields for signal detection. Magnetic Induction sensors operate by inducing voltages in conductive materials via changing magnetic fields. Other types may include fluxgate and soft magnetic sensors, each offering unique applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
