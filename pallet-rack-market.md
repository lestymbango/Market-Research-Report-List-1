<p><p>The pallet rack market encompasses various types to optimize storage efficiency. Selective pallet racks provide direct access to each pallet, maximizing versatility. Drive-in and drive-through racks allow multiple pallet placement in a single lane, enhancing space utilization. Push-back racks enable pallets to slide back for easy access, promoting rotation. Pallet flow racks utilize gravity to move pallets, ensuring first-in-first-out inventory management. Other market types may include cantilever racks and mobile racks, catering to diverse storage needs across industries.</p></p>
