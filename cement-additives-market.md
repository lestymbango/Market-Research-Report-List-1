<p><ul><li>Chemical</li><li>Mineral</li><li>Fibre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cement additives market comprises three primary types: chemical, mineral, and fiber additives. Chemical additives enhance cement performance by improving workability, reducing water demand, and accelerating curing times. Mineral additives, such as fly ash and silica fume, enhance durability and strengthen the cement matrix while contributing to sustainability. Fiber additives, including steel and polypropylene fibers, improve tensile strength and prevent cracking. Together, these additives play a crucial role in optimizing cement properties for various construction applications.</p></p>
