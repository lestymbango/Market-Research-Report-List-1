<p><ul><li>Two-plate Injection Mold</li><li>Three-plate Injection Mould</li><li>Stack Injection Mold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The injection mold market is categorized into three main types: two-plate, three-plate, and stack injection molds. Two-plate molds consist of a simple structure with two halves, ideal for parts with minimal complexity. Three-plate molds have an additional plate for improved part removal and are suitable for complex designs. Stack injection molds feature multiple cavities in a stacked arrangement, enhancing production efficiency and output. Each type caters to specific manufacturing needs, impacting costs, quality, and turnaround times in production.</p></p>
