<p><p>A manual honey extractor is a device used by beekeepers to extract honey from honeycombs without damaging the comb structure. It typically operates through a hand-crank mechanism that spins the frames, allowing centrifugal force to draw honey out while leaving the comb intact for reuse. These extractors are popular among small-scale beekeepers and hobbyists due to their affordability, simplicity, and ease of use.</p><p>The Manual Honey Extractor Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising awareness of the health benefits of honey and increased interest in beekeeping as a sustainable hobby.
