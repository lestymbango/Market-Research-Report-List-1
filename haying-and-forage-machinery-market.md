<p><p>The haying and forage machinery market encompasses various equipment types essential for hay production. Mowers are used for cutting grass and legumes, while conditioners help dry the cut forage by breaking down stems. Tedders aerate the cut grass, promoting even drying. Balers compress the dried forage into bales for storage and transport. Additional equipment includes rakes and forks, which assist in gathering and handling forage. Each type plays a crucial role in the efficiency of hay-making operations.</p></p>
