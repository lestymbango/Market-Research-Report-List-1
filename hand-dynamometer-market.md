<p><p>The hand dynamometer market is primarily divided into two types: electronic medical dynamometers and mechanical medical dynamometers. Electronic medical dynamometers utilize digital technology to precisely measure grip strength, often featuring memory functions and data analysis capabilities for better patient monitoring and assessment. In contrast, mechanical medical dynamometers employ traditional spring-based systems, providing direct readings through analog gauges. Both types serve essential roles in rehabilitation and strength testing, catering to different clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
