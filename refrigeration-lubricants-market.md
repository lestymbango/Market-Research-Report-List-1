<p><p>The refrigeration lubricants market comprises three main types: mineral oil lubricants, synthetic lubricants, and other lubricants. Mineral oil lubricants are derived from refining crude oil, offering good performance at a lower cost, making them popular for traditional systems. Synthetic lubricants, on the other hand, are engineered from chemical compounds, providing superior thermal stability, better lubrication properties, and compatibility with modern refrigerants. Other lubricants may include bio-based or specialty products that cater to specific applications or environmental standards, offering unique benefits.</p></p>
