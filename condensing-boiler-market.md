<p><p>The condensing boiler market can be categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical condensing boilers are designed to optimize space by standing upright, making them ideal for installations where floor space is limited. They often feature a compact design and can be efficiently stacked. In contrast, horizontal condensing boilers are laid out flat, allowing for easier maintenance access and typically handling larger heating demands. Both types effectively utilize heat recovery processes to enhance energy efficiency.</p></p>
