<p><ul><li>Audio Line Drivers</li><li>Audio Line Receivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio line market is divided into two primary segments: audio line drivers and audio line receivers. Audio line drivers are responsible for converting audio signals into amplified electrical signals suitable for transmission over longer distances, ensuring high fidelity and minimal distortion. Conversely, audio line receivers take these transmitted signals and convert them back into perceptible audio, maintaining quality and clarity. These components are essential for various applications, including professional sound systems, home theatres, and communication devices, facilitating seamless audio transmission and reception.</p></p>
