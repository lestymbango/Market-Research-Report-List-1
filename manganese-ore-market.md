<p><p>The manganese ore market can be segmented into three main types: lump, particles, and others. Particles, which are finer and often produced during processing, are typically used in applications requiring specific particle sizes. The "others" category includes various forms such as manganese oxide or ferromanganese, catering to specialized industrial applications and chemical production. Each type plays a distinct role in the overall manganese ore supply chain.</p></p>
