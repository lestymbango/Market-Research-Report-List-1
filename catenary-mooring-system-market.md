<p><ul><li>Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA)</li><li>Vertical Load Anchors (VLA)</li><li>Suction Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The catenary mooring system market features various anchor types, primarily including Drag Embedment Anchors (DEA), Vertical Load Anchors (VLA), and Suction Anchors. DEAs rely on their weight and drag force for stability, suitable for softer seabeds. VLAs transfer vertical loads through a vertical shaft, providing enhanced holding capacity in rocky conditions. Suction Anchors utilize submerged excavation to create a negative pressure, allowing for substantial holding power, making them ideal for deep-water applications. Each type caters to specific environmental conditions and operational requirements.</p></p>
