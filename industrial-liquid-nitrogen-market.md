<p><strong>The Industrial Liquid Nitrogen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cryogenic Fractional Distillated</li><li>Pressure Swing Adsorpted</li><li>Membrane Separated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial liquid nitrogen market is categorized into three primary types: cryogenic fractional distillation, pressure swing adsorption, and membrane separation. Cryogenic fractional distillation involves cooling air to separate nitrogen from other gases based on boiling points. Pressure swing adsorption uses selective adsorption materials to capture nitrogen under high pressure, releasing impurities. Membrane separation employs a selective permeable membrane to separate nitrogen from other gases based on molecular size. Each method offers varying efficiency and cost-effectiveness, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
